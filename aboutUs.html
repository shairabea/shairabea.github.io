About Us

Elijah and Diana took over their father's printing business when the latter retired.
They're expert printers and binders, and have delivered several outstanding award-
winning print documents that rival the beauty and quality of French expert Jean Montruile.

Their sisters, Angela, Lovely and Shaira, are the world-renown restaurateurs educated
in the The Brasaille Foundation of Switzerland. They created several signature dishes
of the Hilton hotels worldwide.

Elijah, Diana, Angela, Lovely and Shaira will put together a special place where people
can have their documents printed, and still relax and enjoy. It is a true marriage of both
expertises.
